EDI Integration – B2B Shop & Dropshipment

This page provides a high-level overview of Mascot’s EDI integration options, to help you choose the right setup before moving on to the technical documentation.

Overview

The same EDI foundation supports both B2B Shop and Dropshipment. The difference lies in how goods are delivered and how your business operates.

B2B Shop

The B2B Shop setup is intended for retailers purchasing goods to their own warehouse.

  • Delivery to retailer address
  • Retailer owns and manages inventory
  • Orders and invoices exchanged via EDI
Dropshipment

Dropshipment allows you to sell products without holding stock by shipping directly to your customer.

  • Delivery directly to end customer
  • No inventory held by retailer
  • Order, delivery status, and invoicing via EDI
B2B Shop vs Dropshipment – Comparison
Area B2B Shop Dropshipment
Delivery destination Retailer warehouse End customer
Inventory Held by retailer Held by Mascot
Order source ERP or B2B webshop E-commerce platform or ERP
Fulfilment Mascot → Retailer Mascot → End customer
EDI scope Orders, delivery info, invoices Orders, delivery info, invoices
Typical use case Stock-based wholesale Extended assortment, no stock risk
